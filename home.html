BASIC SUMMARY

About Cyber Security

Cyber Security is the technology that defends people’s private information on the internet from attacks and people who are illegally trying to access their data. When exploring this website, you will learn more on how AI will help deveope cyber security technology, what careers you may find in this field, and even examples of the technology being used today.

What goes into Cyber Security


AI is going to hold a very big part in cyber security and will most likely be used by both cyber criminals and cyber security teams. This technology will soon be the only way to prevent and/or support attacks as the complexity grows in both areas. For example, AI will be used to identify “fake news” across social media platforms and inform people interested in the information that the new their reading is invalid.
